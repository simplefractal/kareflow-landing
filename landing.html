How It Works

1. Patient is discharged.

We integrate with the hospital's system so that an alert is triggered as soon as your patient is discharged.

2. Dashboard is updated.

The patient automatically is added to the dashboard. The dashboard contains the list of patients to follow up, filtered by soonest deadline.

3. Staff follows up.

Office staff goes through the list on the dashboard and calls the patients that need to be called and schedules a visit for the relevant patients.

Benefits

Follow up with patients sooner.

This will allow you to see the patient sooner after discharge, reconcile medications and ensure the patient's adherence and good health.

Get paid more by Medicare.

With the new Transitional Care Management (TCM) codes, if a patient is called within 2 days of discharge and seen within 7 or 14 days, depending on the severity of the case, the doctor is eligible to bill for $230 for the higher-level code and $160 for lower-level code.
